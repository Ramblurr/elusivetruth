What's this all about?

  1. Well that's the question isn't it? Answer that question and you'll be able to solve all the world's problems. Or maybe not. I sure don't know the answer, so you won't find it here.

  2. Woah, not so philosophical now!

  3. Sorry, I get like that occasionally. My thoughts tend to wander. Speaking of wandering, that's what I do.

  4. Wander? What do you mean?

  5. I guess you could call it traveling, as my wandering looks sort of like that. I've been on the road in one form or another since 2006.

    But it's not really traveling, you see. I'm not going anywhere.

  6. How can you be traveling but not going anywhere?

  7. I'm not traveling, that's the thing. I'm moving. I love to move, I can't stay still. Some might call it a compulsion, they might not be wrong. Movement is my life.

  8. Where are you moving?

  9. Ah! Now this is is the question, enough of the vagaries of the mind and the abstract! Life is in the world and full of the unexpected, the unanticipated, the unimagined.

    You can find where I'm living life on my map. Currently I am traveling east.

  10. East? East from where? To where?

  11. Well in January 2013, I found myself in the Netherlands along the North Sea coast. I began heading east by biycle and raft. I'm over in Eastern Europe now, nominally heading east. Not "to" anywhere, just east.

  12. Bicycle, raft?!

  13. Yeah, a few years ago in South America I discovered a passion for slow-travel. What better slow-travel is there than the human-powered kind? The bicycle allows me to carry everything I need to be mostly self-sufficient on the road.

    Since then I've picked up an inflatable packraft that I can pack on the bike, and stow the bike on. The raft allows me to navigate lakes, rivers and, maybe even seas.
